# In search of the energyRt logo

“The energyRt project is in the process of searching for its
logo-identity, which reflects its name (/ˈɛnərdʒi ɑrt/), purpose (macro
energy systems modeling, long-term energy landscapes, energy transition,
and decarbonization), and modular object-oriented workflow (similar to
LEGO blocks or a jigsaw puzzle). Additionally, the logo will follow the
hexagon conventions commonly used in R logos.”

In this age of AI, with tools like ChatGPT and DALL-E, why not tap into
the collective knowledge of human creativity? That’s exactly what we
did. While none of the AI-generated logos are perfect straight out of
the box—given the current state of AI—they do offer intriguing
perspectives and design ideas. As part of an ongoing, iterative process,
the most promising prototypes will serve as energyRt logo for a week,
before being replaced by another one, until the final design is chosen.

If you’d like to share your own logo idea, feel free to submit it via
[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/optimal2050/energyRt/issues) or send
it by
[email](https://energyRt.org/articles/optimal2050@optimalsolution.dev).
